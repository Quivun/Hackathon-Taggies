About Taggies

Taggies is a virtual space where Aggies can create online profiles to reach out and connect with each other. In light of recent circumstances, several Aggies learning remotely find it difficult to connect with their peers. Not only that, but some Aggies on campus may be shy or feel uncomfortable meeting new people in person. Due to the lack of in-person interactions, Taggies was created with the intention of bringing the Aggie family closer together whether they are at home or on campus. It's easy to start, just click the Sign Up button at the top of the page to create your very own tag and start meeting your fellow Aggies!
